== Description ==

# Fast, Simple Satisfaction Surveys
## Create survey forms on your site. Ask for feedback. Improve.

This plugin is an integration plugin and is relying on a 3rd party
forms service: [Insight Stash](https://insightstash.com)

Insight Stash is a customer feedback management platform, it helps businesses
understand their visitors and customers better. With Insight Stash you will improve customer
experience, gather feedback from users, understand pain points, hunt
bugs on site, capture emails and much much more.

Features:

1. Launch custom surveys on your site
1. Email surveys
1. NPS, Satisfaction, Bug Reports, Experience surveys, Lead forms
1. Custom styling
1. Rules and Steps
1. Served through CDN
1. Advanced reports
1. Reporting API integration
1. Scheduled Exports
1. Addons

https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=ze0JcuYhV3E

== Installation ==

1. Upload `insight-stash.php` to the `/wp-content/plugins/` directory
2. Activate the plugin through the 'Plugins' menu in WordPress
3. Enter Account ID on the plugin options page
4. Now you can launch and control surveys on your site through administration panel: https://app.insightstash.com
